Dennis Rumohr's Final Project one Pager

A web experience showcasing the broad work, broad products, and experience of Dennis Rumohr

Dennis Rumohr's Sanctuary will focus on sharing selected work, projects, and publications from Dennis Rumohr, with an emphasis on showcasing his work as a creative director and in other design leadership roles. The site will also be the primary marketing outlet for his future commodities and serve as a secondary promotion to his future website creations. Although not currently updating the site or the site being automatically updated, Dennis Rumohr's Sanctuary must be able to "switch" into a mode that communicates to visitors Dennis's daily thoughts or whatever news that he finds important.
